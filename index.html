Hi! I am a second year PhD student at UC Berkeley working with Sergey Levine. I previously did my undergraduate degree at the University of Toronto advised by Tim Barfoot. My research focuses on generalist robot navigation and interfacing with robots through natural language. I am also interested in mobile manipulation and pushing the boundaries of leveraging foundation models and non-robot data to improve robot learning.

I am interested in internships for Summer 2025 so please contact me with opportunities you think may be a good fit!
